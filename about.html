About Me

My name is Alda Boyd. I'm a 20 year old Nigerian-American visual artist from Dallas, Texas. I have a creative backround in VR, AR and projection mapping. In addition to my passion for technology, I love to learn and teach others. I possess strong leadership and communication skills, so I work well in group settings.

Here you will find an archive of my work over the past 3 years attending Parsons School of Design along with my works in progress and collaborations. In my art I like to explore themes relating to future technologies, transhumanism, communication and emotion.
